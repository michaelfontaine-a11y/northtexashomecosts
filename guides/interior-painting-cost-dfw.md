## What moves the price

- Ceiling height — 9-ft ceilings add roughly 12% to a room's cost, 10-ft about 20%, 12-ft about 30%, and 14-ft vaulted ceilings can double the labor; painters typically add $100–$500 per room for ladder/scaffold work above 10 ft
- Wall condition and prep — patching, sanding, and texture repair add $0.50–$0.75 per sq ft in older Dallas homes; heavy prep can add $500–$2,000 to a whole-home job
- Paint quality — budget brands run $20–$30/gallon, standard Sherwin-Williams/Benjamin Moore lines $40–$55, premium paints $100+; Dallas pros average about $74/gallon on installed jobs
- Color changes and extra coats — dark-to-light conversions need three coats plus primer; an extra coat adds $330–$970 per room in Dallas
- Labor rates — DFW painters charge $56–$83/hour, below the ~$92 national average; specialty work like cabinet spraying runs up to $117/hour
- Scope add-ons — painting trim adds about 20% to a room's labor; ceilings add $1–$2 per sq ft on top of wall pricing
